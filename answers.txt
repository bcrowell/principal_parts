πόρον
The present is not used, so πόρω is only a dictionary form. (Sometimes also listed under πορεῖν.) The aorist is to offer, perfect to be fated. Homer uses mostly the 3s aorist πόρεν and the perfect participle πεπρωμένον. He uses the finite perfect only once, as πέπρωοται.
  offer; be fated
